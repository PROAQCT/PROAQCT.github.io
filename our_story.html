Our Mission

Our mission is to proactively deliver safe and optimal patient-specific imaging. Since 2018, ProAqCT has successfully leveraged radiomics, state-of-the-art organ dose library matrices, and machine learning to provide patient specific imaging recommendations that reduce radiation risk and optimize image quality. Our mission is to revolutionize patient imaging by combining advanced physics with high-performance computation to provide a safer, higher-quality, and more informed diagnosis.

Why Quality Imaging Matters To Us

Too often, patient imaging protocols rely on the “average” patient, rather than the individual. This leads to a one-size-fits-all approach to imaging which sacrifices quality and risks introducing unnecessary clinical errors and adverse patient outcomes.

To change this, we designed advanced features that use dose and protocol data to evaluate the quality and safety of each patient study to ensure that every exam delivers the best possible diagnostic outcome. Our goal is to facilitate and ensure safe and optimized imaging for every, single patient.

Improving Patient Care & Quality

With our cutting-edge technology, ProAqCT has optimized the quality of hundreds of thousands of patient examinations, ensuring safer and superior images for patients nationwide. We have also been instrumental in helping numerous healthcare institutions maintain regulatory standards and compliance for monitoring patient doses. Our solution is fully aligned with all patient dose monitoring regulations and simplifies dose reporting to most accrediting bodies such as ACR, DICOE, DIR, and LEAPFROG.
