Optimization Delivery Service

Our team of qualified medical physicists are dedicated to helping you achieve your quality, safety, and compliance goals. Our cutting-edge insights and innovative tools enable us to collaborate with your imaging team and provide valuable recommendations on how to enhance the performance of your clinical protocols. We generate detailed reports that compare your dose performance to external benchmarks, identify patient exams that exceed alert thresholds, and track the performance of your most common protocols over time. These reports are user-friendly and designed to help you continuously improve your radiation dose management and stay in compliance with regulatory requirements. Our dose optimization service is customized to meet your clinical needs and focused on delivering the best possible outcomes for your patients.
