Meet our Team!


Larry Cumberbatch Ph.D

CEO

Dr. Cumberbatch is a Medical Physicist with over 10 years of clinical experience. He earned his Ph. D from Duke University and did his post-doctoral training in machine learning. Having worked with all commercially available Dose Management systems, Dr. Cumberbatch saw an immediate need to improve patient dose management and make it smarter and easier to use. Thanks to his leadership and experience, ProAQCT is the most advanced and user-friendly dose management and imaging informatics solution on the market.

Mel Hebert MS

VP of Sales

Mr. Hebert is a Health Physicist with over 30 years of experience in Nuclear and Radiation Safety. Mel possesses a comprehensive background in regulatory compliance and Radiation Safety derived from serving in state and federal Agencies as a Radiation Safety Officer. He oversees the ProAQCT regulatory compliance program and ensures that ProAQCT meets all QC and radiation dose monitoring guidelines. He also serves as our VP of sales and builds business strategies that position ProAQCT as the world's leading informatics solution.
